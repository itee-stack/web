About Us

I.TEE CONCEPT GLOBAL is a forward-thinking company specializing in smart hospitality solutions and digital transformation (hospitality digital upgrade services, system that pays for itself in few months of implementation). We proudly serve a diverse range of clientele including hotels, lounges, restaurants, banquet facilities and many more industries, delivering innovative technology tailored to elevate guest and customer experiences and streamline service delivery.

We also build advanced Voice Flow Agents and AI chatbots for business, hospitality, and events. Our solutions enable seamless, natural conversations with guests and customers—handling reservations, answering FAQs, taking orders, and providing instant support 24/7. These AI-powered agents can be integrated into your website, WhatsApp, social media, or on-premise kiosks, helping you automate routine tasks, reduce staff workload, and deliver a premium, personalized experience at scale.

We offer basic-to-premium ordering system setup, upgrades, and training. Our mission is to enhance operational efficiency and guest satisfaction by aligning cutting-edge technology with your establishment’s core values—attentiveness, personalization, and guest comfort. With deep expertise in digital systems and smart service platforms, we provide customized solutions designed to meet the unique needs of each client.
